Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our borewell services

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We provide 24/7 emergency response and can typically reach your location within 2-4 hours depending on the area in Hyderabad.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free site assessment and detailed quotations for all our services with no hidden charges.

What areas do you serve in Hyderabad?

We serve all major areas of Hyderabad including Kukatpally, Gachibowli, Hitech City, LB Nagar, Secunderabad, and surrounding regions of Telangana.

How long does borewell drilling take?

Depending on the depth and geological conditions, borewell drilling typically takes 1-3 days. We provide accurate timelines during our initial assessment.

Do you offer warranty on your services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty on all our services. The warranty period varies by service type, which we discuss before starting work.

What makes your borewell scanning different?

We use advanced HD waterproof cameras with real-time monitoring capabilities, providing detailed video reports and accurate problem identification.
