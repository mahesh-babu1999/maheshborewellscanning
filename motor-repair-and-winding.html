What is Motor Repair and Winding?

Motor repair and winding involves rewiring the copper coil, replacing damaged parts, and restoring the electrical and mechanical efficiency of your motor. It ensures smooth operation, power efficiency, and longer life for submersible, monoblock, and openwell motors.
