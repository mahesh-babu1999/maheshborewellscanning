What is New Motor Installation?

New motor installation involves the complete setup of submersible or surface motors for borewells, water tanks, and pumping systems. Our expert technicians ensure perfect alignment, proper wiring, waterproof connections, and full safety measures for efficient and long-lasting performance.

1

Site Inspection & Assessment

Our team visits your site to check bore depth, water level, and existing system setup to determine the right motor type and capacity.

2

Motor Selection

We help you select a suitable motor (submersible or surface type) based on borewell specifications, flow rate needs, and power availability.

3

Preparation & Safety Setup

The motor is prepared with waterproof cable connections, proper winding checks, and sealed joints to prevent leakage or electrical faults.

4

Installation & Fitting

The motor is carefully lowered into the borewell using lifting tools and mounted securely with delivery pipes and safety ropes.

5

Electrical & Pipe Connections

All power cables, pipes, and fittings are connected using durable accessories. The setup is tested for voltage stability and insulation.

6

Testing & Final Inspection

We perform full operational testing to verify smooth performance, water flow, and safety before final handover.
